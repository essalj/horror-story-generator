--- ## Writing Guidelines:

• Maintain language suitable for 7th grade reading level
• Show don't tell, especially for new technology and concepts
• Each chapter should be 3,000-4,000 words
• Balance action, character development, and world-building
• Ensure all setup pays off in satisfying ways
• Keep scientific explanations plausible but accessible
• Maintain consistent character voices and motivations throughout
• Each chapter must end with a compelling hook leading to the next

## Thematic Elements to Weave Throughout:

• Identity and consciousness (Erik/Ragnar integration)
• Unity vs. division (Social Schism resolution)
• Sacrifice for the greater good
• The power of human connection over cold logic
• Legacy and how our actions echo through time
• Choice between expedient solutions and principled stands
